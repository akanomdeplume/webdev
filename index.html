About Skinwalker Ranch

Skinwalker Ranch, Previously known as Sherman Ranch is one of the most paranormal locations on earth, Its just southeast of Ballard, Utah. The land is claimed to have been cursed by the Navajo tribe, with countless claims of skinwalkers roaming the property, all 4 owners the ranch have reported terrifying paranormal activity on the ranch, from strange lights in the sky to tall creatures staring in their windows at night. This is a dream for anyone with an interest in the paranomarl, there are countless campsites in the desert surrounding the ranch, With rich native american culture in the cities around. And if your not into the paranormal the landscape surround the ranch is beautiful, its distant from society with gives you a wonderful place to take a break from life. The nearest city also has a really cool skatepark in the middle of a desert which is an awesome location for a skatepark! And theres a wonderful resturant nearby called UTE Crossing Grill which serves Delicous food including chicken fried steak!
